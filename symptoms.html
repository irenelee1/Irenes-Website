Symptoms of Type 1 Diabetes & Symptoms of Type 2 Diabetes

  • Increased thirst
  • Increased hunger
  • Weight loss
  • Fatigue
  • Blurred vision

Symptoms of Gestational diabetes

  • Overweight and obesity
  • Lack of physical activity
  • You pee more than usual
